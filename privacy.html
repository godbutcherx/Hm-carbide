2. Data Collected Automatically

When you visit our website, our servers temporarily record the following technical data in log files:

  • IP address (anonymised after collection)

  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Referring URL
  • Pages accessed and time spent

This data is used solely for the purpose of ensuring stable and secure operation of the website and for anonymous statistical analysis. No connection is made between this data and any specific individual, and no personal user profiles are created.

3. Cookies

Our website uses cookies — small text files stored on your device by your browser. Cookies help us provide a functional and improved browsing experience.

Essential Cookies

These cookies are required for the basic functionality of the website (e.g. storing your language and theme preference). They cannot be disabled as the site would not function properly without them. No personal data is collected through these cookies.

Analytics Cookies (Google Analytics)

With your consent, we use Google Analytics, a web analytics service provided by Google Ireland Limited ("Google"), Gordon House, Barrow Street, Dublin 4, Ireland.

Google Analytics uses cookies to collect anonymous information about how visitors interact with our website. The data collected includes pages visited, session duration, and approximate geographic region. All IP addresses are anonymised before any data is transmitted to Google's servers.

IP anonymisation is enabled on this website. Your IP address is truncated within the European Economic Area before being transmitted to Google. Only in exceptional circumstances is a full IP address sent to a Google server in the USA and shortened there.

Google will use this information to evaluate your use of the website, compile reports on website activity, and provide other services relating to internet usage. Google may transfer this data to third parties where required by law or where such parties process data on Google's behalf.

You may prevent cookies from being stored by adjusting your browser settings. Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of certain parts of this website. You may also opt out of Google Analytics tracking at any time via the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on.

More information on how Google handles data can be found in Google's Privacy Policy.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

Data processing on this website is carried out on the following legal bases under GDPR:

  • Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR — Legitimate interests (server log files, site security)
  • Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR — Consent (analytics cookies, where applicable)
  • Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR — Performance of a contract (contact form enquiries)

5. Data Retention

Server log files are automatically deleted after 30 days. Data submitted via the contact form is retained only for as long as necessary to respond to your enquiry and fulfil any legal retention obligations. Analytics data is retained in aggregated, anonymised form and is not linked to any individual.

6. Your Rights

Under the GDPR and revDSG, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access — request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification — request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure — request deletion of your data where legally permitted
  • Right to restriction — request that we limit how we use your data
  • Right to data portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Right to object — object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Right to withdraw consent — withdraw any previously given consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at info@hmcarbide.ch. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the competent supervisory authority. In Switzerland, this is the Federal Data Protection and Information Commissioner (FDPIC).
